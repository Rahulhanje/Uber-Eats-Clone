About uber Eats

Uber Eats is a leading online food delivery platform that connects people with a wide variety of restaurants, cafes, and food establishments in their local area. Launched by Uber in 2013, Uber Eats has revolutionized the way people order food by providing a seamless and convenient delivery experience..

At Uber Eats, our mission is to make ordering food as easy as possible for everyone, whether you're at home, in the office, or on the go. We strive to provide a diverse selection of cuisines, ranging from local favorites to popular chains, ensuring there's something for every taste and craving.
